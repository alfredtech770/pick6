PICK6 / DECK 11 / 14 — ETHICS
Responsibility

Responsibility and Ethical Commitment.

Beyond its core activities, Pick6 is firmly committed to a rigorous ethical approach based on transparency, integrity, and respect for its users — promoting an informed and responsible approach to sports analysis.

The company positions itself as a sports prediction advisory platform — not a betting operator — dedicated to providing tools to enrich understanding of sports and support thoughtful decision-making.

This consultative vocation distinguishes it from traditional online gaming operators; Pick6 neither offers nor encourages betting, but reflection and learning. It places particular emphasis on the education of its users and fosters a healthy sports community.

Concrete measures include a strict adult age limit and an in-development reinforced ethical framework with adapted protection mechanisms.

01

Clear Positioning

Expert analyses to inform enthusiast decisions — advisory, not gaming.

02

Protection Measures

Strict adult age limit and risk-awareness messages integrated across the platform.

Awareness tools and protection mechanisms tailored to the model’s specificities.
