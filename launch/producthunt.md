Hey Product Hunt 👋

I'm [your name], building Pick1 — an AI sports prediction engine.

The pitch in one line: **what Kalshi has built for politics, what Polymarket has built for news — but for sports.**

Sports is the world's largest category of probabilistic bets, but the prediction layer has been missing. Sportsbooks have it internally (their lines are predictions), but the actual probabilistic forecast is hidden behind the price. Tipsters fill the vacuum on Telegram by selling "locks" for $200/mo and quietly archiving the misses.

Pick1 is the AI-native answer:

🎯 **Calibrated probabilities, not opinions** — every pick has a confidence score from 0–100% that's actually calibrated. A 75% pick wins 75% of the time over a long sample.

📊 **Public ledger** — every pick (winning OR losing) is timestamped and auto-logged. No selective sharing. No "look at this cashed parlay" without seeing the misses.

🌍 **9 sports** — NBA, NFL, EPL, MLB, UFC, NHL, F1, tennis, cricket. One subscription.

🧮 **Calibrated against the closing line** — model is recalibrated nightly on closing-line value (CLV), the gold-standard metric pro bettors and sportsbooks both use.

We're pre-launch with a waitlist — first 100 signups get closed beta a week before public, and every signup gets a free week at launch via WhatsApp.

Methodology + comparison vs Kalshi/Polymarket: pick1.live/methodology

Happy to answer anything — about the model, the calibration, the comparison to prediction markets, or why we're skeptical of tipsters. AMA.
