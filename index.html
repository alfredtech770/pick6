FAQ.

Can I cancel anytime?
Yes — cancel from Settings or the App Store at any time. You'll keep Pro access until the end of your billing period.
What's included in the 7-day trial?
Full Pro access — unlimited picks, all sports, live tracking, full analytics. No charge until day 7. Cancel before then to avoid any charge.
Can I switch between weekly and monthly?
Yes — switch plans at any time from Settings. The new rate takes effect on your next billing cycle.
How accurate is the AI?
Pick6's models hit ~68% across the major US sports over the last 12 months, audited monthly. Confidence varies by sport and by pick — every prediction comes with its own confidence score so you know when the model is sure and when it isn't.
Does Pick6 take bets?
No. Pick6 is a research and prediction app — we surface picks, you place them at your sportsbook of choice. We compare lines across DraftKings, FanDuel, BetMGM, Caesars and bet365 so you always get the best price.
What sports do you cover?
NBA, NFL, MLB, NHL, EPL (and all major soccer leagues), UFC, F1, ATP/WTA tennis, and IPL/international cricket. New sports are added quarterly — Pro members get early access.
